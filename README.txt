Keyboard Scrambler scrambles the keymap of the keys randomly
every 20 seconds. 

To return to your regular keymap, click "Revert to Normal" in the application
Or, end the application and in the commandline enter:

	cd C:\
	taskkill /f /im wscript.exe
	taskkill /f /im autohotkey.exe

This will kill all running scripts and revert all key settings back to normal.

Use the keyboard scrambler to protect your computer while you're away, or use
it to prank your friends! Hope you enjoy it.
